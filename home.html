    ABOUT US
    Dear Benefactors and Well-wishers
    The American College with the motto of Purificatus Non Consumptus, had its inception in the year 1881. As a pioneer in the field of collegiate education, it provides quality and affordable arts and science education. Among all other departments, the Department of Commerce stands out as the brightest star in the cosmos of the college. The Commerce Department of The American college was incorporated in the year 1947. The Department has been serving the student community for over 70 years. Every year, about 450 students in total, graduate from various wings of the department . The college has a vivid strength of over 12,000+ students with the Department of Commerce occupying a predominant position with 2383 students. Every year during the Department's annual cultural fest participants spread across South India hop in to join the grandiose occasion. This Department has been catering to students with ambition and zeal in and around the city of Madurai. Many scholars have blossomed from this department to become entrepreneurs and business icons.The Department strives to reach greater heights in the future. The Commerce Department has its own association of which every professor and student are members. The Commerce association hosts an intercollegiate meet every year to colebrate its union. The name of this grandiose occasion is COMMAS. Around 50 institutions across South India participate in this event to make it more joyous. The success of this event is based on the financial aid received every year from business entreprencurs in and around Madurai, the hard work of all the members of the association COMMAS ‘24 - the grand slams makes its grand return conferring us with delight and phenomenal activities inculcating everyone blast with innovations to excel. Amidst the traditional temple city where lies an oasis built with the majestic colonial red bricks, flavoured with sprinkled collage of colours from the lush green gardens, inviting a solace of chirping birds, we have set the stage for COMMAS ‘24 with the back drop of nature and tradition COMMAS is here! Come and drench yourself in a shower the fun and excitement with our prolific patrons! Come, shed your Camouflage! Bring out your expertise! We are waiting with our arms open!
